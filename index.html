About Us

Inspiration Behind YeromRecipe Hub

Our journey began in the cozy corners of our kitchens, amidst the clattering of pots and pans, and the aromatic allure of home-cooked meals. Fueled by our passion for food and technology, we embarked on creating the YeromRecipe Hub. What started as a conversation over a homemade meal, has now blossomed into a project that we are proud to present as part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
